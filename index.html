Kinds of Video Games

RPG - these are when players are tasked to play the role of a fictional character in a fictional setting,they are then incharge of the action taken by said character

MOBA - these are a genre of video games in that of which a team of player battle against another

Sandbox - these are a type of games that give the players creative freedom in its digital world,these games usually don't have a linear progression/goal to reach

Rogue-like - these are a subgenre of RPG and have the player traverse a dungeon type setting with procedurally generated rooms,the goal in these games is to survive for as long as you can since death is mostly permanent
